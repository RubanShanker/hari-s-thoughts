Paganism vs Hinduism

by Hari Shanker 01 Jan, 2022 3 min read

Paganism vs Hinduism. Similarities: Pagans were also polytheists like Hindus. They worshipped multiple god's like Odin, Thor, Freyr, Freya etc., Likewise Hindus worshipped many gods Shiva, Vishnu, Kali etc,,

Pagans were holding their cultural identity strongly even after 700-900 years after the birth of Jesus (i.e they haven't converted to Christianity up until 900 A.D), eventhough their neighbours converted.

Likewise Hindus in India had strong cultural roots and until the arrival of Vascoda Gama they never know that there is a religion called Christianity.

And it's only during the time of British Empire some minor population of Hindus converted to Christianity for getting benefits from their English masters ( mostly for the sake of getting food) Most importantly pagans followed Idol worship like that of Hindus. They had statues of their gods placed inside the temple. Most importantly odin the All father's statue was placed for worship.
